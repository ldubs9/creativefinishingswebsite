How It Works

Our Simple Process

01

Consultation & Measurement

We visit your space, take precise measurements, and discuss your vision — materials, finishes, and timeline. No obligation, no pressure.

02

Design & Sample Approval

We present finish samples and a detailed quote. Choose your wrap from hundreds of premium options, and approve before we begin.

03

Professional Installation

Our skilled installers handle everything — surface prep, precision application, and seamless edge finishing. Most residential jobs completed in 1–2 days.

04

Final Inspection & Warranty

We walk through the finished space with you, ensure everything is perfect, and provide a full warranty on materials and labour.
